---
title: Practical travel preparation tips for first-time international movers
date: 2026-04-28
category: Travel Tips
excerpt: Document, budget, and arrival planning essentials for smoother first-month settlement.
---
First-time international movers should prepare a three-part plan: legal documents, financial setup, and arrival logistics. Keep digital and printed copies of key records, including passport, visa approvals, accommodation confirmations, and insurance documents.

Budget planning should cover at least 6–8 weeks of initial costs, including transport, deposits, SIM/data, and emergency reserves. Track mandatory registration timelines in your destination city.

A soft-landing checklist helps reduce stress: airport pickup options, temporary housing confirmation, and a first-week schedule for banking, registration, and campus or employer orientation.
